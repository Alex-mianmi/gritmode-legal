This Privacy Policy does not govern separate data practices of third parties such as Apple, the App Store, or email service providers unless expressly stated otherwise.

3. What Data We Collect and Process

3.1. Data You Provide Directly

GritMode does not require account creation and may be used without registration.

However, we may process data that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • your name and email address if you contact support;
  • the content of your request;
  • attachments, screenshots, or other materials you send to us.

3.2. Habit, Task, Progress, and Content Data

When you use GritMode, the app locally processes and stores data that you create within the app, such as:

  • habits, tasks, goals, and related settings;
  • completion records, streaks, schedules, and reminders;
  • progress, activity, and feature statistics;
  • notes, entries, and other data you type into the app;
  • workout session data, time balance data, and unlock events, where such features are used.

This data is used to provide the core functionality of the app and is stored locally on your device. We do not access this data and do not transmit it to our servers.

3.3. Data from Device Features and Permissions

Depending on the features you enable, GritMode may process the following categories of data locally on the device:

  • HealthKit: for example, step count data if used to award minutes or calculate progress;
  • Screen Time / Family Controls / Device Activity / Managed Settings: data and technical tokens necessary to apply restrictions, blocks, and rules to selected apps;
  • Camera: if the app uses local repetition counting or a similar feature, the camera may be used for on-device image processing. Camera images are not saved and not transmitted off the device;
  • Local notifications: information required to display reminders and notifications from your device.

All such data is processed on the device using Apple's system APIs and is not transmitted to us.

3.4. Device and Technical Data

The app uses technical information on the device that is necessary for functionality, compatibility, and security, such as:

  • device type and model;
  • operating system version;
  • device language and regional settings;
  • app version;
  • time zone;
  • technical identifiers needed for internal app functions.

This information is used to support the correct operation of the app on the device and is not transmitted to us. Separately, information about the status of your subscription is processed through Apple / StoreKit, as described in Section 3.7.

3.5. Diagnostics and Crash Data

At this time, GritMode does not use third-party crash reporting services and does not automatically receive crash diagnostics directly.

Apple may collect anonymous diagnostic information and crash statistics in accordance with your iOS settings ("Analytics & Improvements"). Such processing is governed by Apple's policies.

If you voluntarily send us an error description, screenshots, or other support materials, we may process that information in order to investigate and resolve the issue.

3.6. Analytics

GritMode uses Amplitude (operated by Amplitude, Inc., United States — https://amplitude.com/privacy) as a product analytics provider to understand how the app is used in aggregate and to improve it.

Amplitude processes:

  • a pseudonymous, device-generated analytics identifier (no name, email, or Apple ID);
  • technical information automatically captured by the Amplitude SDK, such as approximate country derived from IP address, device model, operating system version, carrier, app version, and session start / end;
  • product events generated by your use of the app, such as app opens, onboarding completion, paywall views, subscription purchases and restores, completed workout sessions (exercise type, repetition count, duration, earned minutes), and streak start / break events;
  • user-level properties such as "days since first open," app version, and premium status flag.

Amplitude does not receive the content of your habits, tasks, notes, HealthKit data (including step counts), camera images, the list of apps you have selected to block in Screen Time, names, email addresses, or any other personally identifying information.

GritMode does not use advertising SDKs, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or cross-app / cross-site tracking technologies, and does not participate in behavioral advertising.

3.7. Payments and Subscriptions

If GritMode offers subscriptions or in-app purchases, subscription-related information is processed to provide access to the relevant features.

In particular:

  • payments and transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store / StoreKit;
  • we do not receive or store full payment credentials, including bank card details;
  • subscription entitlements are managed through RevenueCat (https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy). RevenueCat receives Apple transaction receipts and a pseudonymous, device-generated app user ID so that premium status can be validated and restored across devices. We do not send health data, habits, workout content, or any other user content to RevenueCat;
  • we may process limited subscription status information required to activate, restore, or confirm premium access.

We do not sell personal data.

  • RevenueCat, Inc. (United States) for subscription entitlement validation and cross-device restoration — see https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy;

In such cases, processing takes place within the infrastructure of those services under their respective data protection frameworks.
